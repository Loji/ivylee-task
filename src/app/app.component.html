Ivy Lee Task List

Close help
Simple program to help you organise your productive todo list.
  1. At the end of the day write down six things you need to do tomorrow. Do not write down more than six things.
  2. When you start working tomorrow focus on most important task. Work until you finish it before starting working on other.
  3. At the end of day mark everything you've done as finished. Unfinished tasks will automatically go to next day.
Method source and inspiration: http://jamesclear.com/ivy-lee
